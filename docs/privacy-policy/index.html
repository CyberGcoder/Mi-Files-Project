Mi Files Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 27, 2026

Mi Files is a local-first Windows and Android file transfer app created by BK Sekulane. This policy explains how Mi Files handles information when you use it to pair devices, browse folders, preview files, stream media, and transfer files between your own devices.

The latest Mi Files Windows installer and portable build are available on GitHub Releases.

How Mi Files works

Mi Files is designed to operate directly between your Android device and your Windows PC, or between paired devices, over your local network or a PC hotspot. Mi Files is not designed as a cloud storage service.

Permissions and device access

  • Camera access may be used to scan QR codes for device pairing when you choose to use the scanner.
  • Nearby Wi-Fi, network, and location-related permissions may be used where Android requires them for local discovery, hotspot joining, or local network communication.
  • Notification access may be used to show transfer progress, approvals, connection status, sharing state, and optional local feature reminders if you leave them enabled.
  • Folder or file access is used only for the folders, files, or media items you choose to browse, preview, save, send, or receive with Mi Files.

Information Mi Files handles

  • Pairing and session data such as local device addresses, pairing tokens, and transport security details.
  • File and folder metadata needed to browse, sort, search, preview, stream, and transfer content.
  • App preferences such as selected folders, favourites, interface settings, transfer history, and local ad state stored on your devices.
  • Hotspot details when you explicitly use hotspot-based pairing.

How Mi Files uses this information

  • To discover and pair your devices.
  • To display file and folder listings inside the app.
  • To complete transfers, previews, streaming, quick share, and approval prompts that you initiate.
  • To remember your settings, favourites, chosen folders, and local ad state.

What Mi Files does not do by default

  • Mi Files does not create a user account for you.
  • Mi Files does not upload your files to a central Mi Files cloud service.
  • Mi Files does not sell your personal data.
  • Mi Files does not use a Mi Files cloud account system for file transfer.

Sharing and disclosure

Mi Files may send files and related transfer metadata directly to the Windows PC or Android device that you explicitly pair and choose to communicate with. These transfers happen to provide the file transfer and preview features you request.

Advertising, consent, and billing

The Android app may display Google AdMob ads, including app-open, banner, interstitial, rewarded, and rewarded interstitial ads. The Windows desktop app does not show third-party advertising. The Android app may also use Google's User Messaging Platform to request advertising consent choices where required. If you choose to buy the optional one-time Google Play remove ads purchase, Google Play Billing may provide purchase status and restore information so Mi Files can honor that entitlement on this Android app.

Google and its partners may process advertising-related data such as device identifiers, app interactions, diagnostics, approximate location signals, consent selections, and other information described in Google's own policies in order to serve, measure, or limit ads. Mi Files may also store ad-related state on-device, such as ad cooldowns, consent state, temporary ad-free rewards granted after an optional rewarded ad or rewarded interstitial ad, and permanent ad-removal entitlement state for the optional Google Play purchase.

Security

Mi Files uses pairing tokens, approval prompts, and transport security features intended to protect transfers between your devices. Sensitive pairing values stored by the app are protected on-device and excluded from normal cloud backup by default.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. You are responsible for pairing only with devices you trust, reviewing transfer requests, using appropriate device security, and keeping your own backups of important data.

Data retention and deletion

  • Pairing data, preferences, favourites, transfer history, and local ad state remain on your device until you remove them, clear app storage, or uninstall the app.
  • Transferred files remain in the folders where you save them until you delete them.
  • If you unpair devices, stop sharing, or choose a different folder, the related active configuration is replaced or cleared on your device.

Your choices

  • You choose which devices to pair.
  • You choose which folders to grant to the app and which transfers to approve.
  • You can choose whether to watch optional rewarded ads or accept an optional rewarded interstitial offer.
  • You can choose whether to keep optional monthly feature reminders enabled on Android.
  • You can choose whether to buy the optional one-time Google Play remove ads purchase for this Android app.
  • You can stop background sharing, unpair devices, clear app storage, or uninstall the app at any time.

Changes to this policy
